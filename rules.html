Game Rules

Welcome to Classic Tic Tac Toe. These are the official rules for this site version.

Objective

Be the first player to place three of your marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row on the 3Ã—3 board.

Players

  • Two players: Player X and Player O.
  • Player X always goes first.

How to Play

  1. Players take turns placing their mark (X or O) in an empty cell on the board.
  2. Do not place more than one mark per turn or overwrite an occupied cell.
  3. The first player to get three marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins.
  4. If all nine cells are filled without either player getting three in a row, the game is a draw.
  5. Use the New Game button to reset the board and start over; scores persist on the page for the session.

Fair Play & Accessibility

Please play fairly and avoid automated scripts or bots. This site is intended for casual, manual play.

Troubleshooting

If the board does not update or you see unexpected behavior, try reloading the page. If problems persist, contact us.
